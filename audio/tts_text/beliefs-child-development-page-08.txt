I have also learned to pay closer attention to the influence of peers. Positive friendships can give a student a sense of belonging, confidence, and opportunities to develop social skills. At the same time, peer pressure, exclusion, and bullying can harm a student’s self-esteem and willingness to participate at school.

This has helped me understand that inclusion means more than placing students in the same classroom. As an EA, I should help create meaningful opportunities for every student to participate, communicate, and build relationships with others.
