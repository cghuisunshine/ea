When I reflect on my learning, I can see that my beliefs have been shaped by my experiences in China, my teaching experience, my observations in Canada, and this program. My earlier experiences taught me the value of effort, responsibility, discipline, and academic achievement. This program has taught me that students also need safety, trusting relationships, belonging, individualized support, and opportunities to become independent.

The most important change in my thinking is that an EA should not try to make every student fit into one system. My role is to help remove barriers so that each student can learn, participate, develop their strengths, and feel that they belong. This is the kind of Education Assistant I hope to become.

Thank you.
