Another important change in my thinking came from learning about neuroplasticity. I once believed that a child’s academic ability was mostly fixed. I now understand that the brain can grow and change through experience, practice, supportive relationships, and education. Repeated positive experiences can strengthen connections in the brain.

This understanding will guide how I support students as an Education Assistant. If a student is struggling today, I should not assume that the student is incapable. With suitable support, encouragement, practice, and time, that student may make significant progress.
