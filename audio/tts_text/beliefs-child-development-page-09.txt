Another important lesson for me is that early challenges can affect development without completely determining a child’s future. Experiences such as poverty, abuse, neglect, family addiction, or trauma may increase the risk of emotional, social, behavioural, or learning difficulties.

At the same time, caring adults, supportive schools, positive friendships, stable routines, and access to services can act as protective factors. As an EA, I may become one of the trusted adults who helps a student feel safe, supported, and capable. This reminds me that even small, consistent actions can make a meaningful difference.
