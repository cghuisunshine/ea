My understanding of an educator’s role has also changed. I once believed that teachers and Education Assistants were mainly responsible for teaching knowledge, maintaining discipline, and helping students achieve good grades.

I now understand that educators also support development by creating safe environments, building trusting relationships, modelling skills, and providing opportunities to practise. In my work as an EA, I may use a visual schedule, divide a task into smaller steps, repeat an instruction, or provide additional time. My goal will be to offer enough support for the student to succeed while still encouraging independence.
